hi. I'm cami!

I am driven by understanding how we learn, play, create, and connect—with each other, with technology, with nature, and with ourselves—within the world at large.

My curiosity about how people think and interact led me to study cognitive science. But, a desire to use that understanding to make meaningful impact led me to design.

As a designer, I am interested in exploring how we might craft interactions and experiences that help people, whether that is creatively, educationally, emotionally, or just for play.
